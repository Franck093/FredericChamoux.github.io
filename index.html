ABOUT ME

Hi,

My name is Frederic Chamoux and I'm your local guide in Nice and its surroundings for tours in Portuguese, Spanish, English or French!

Locally born and raised, I've studied and worked many years in hospitality in Nice at the prestigious Regina and Le Negresco palace hotels. Having spent most of my life in Nice, I know the city and its region like the back of my hand!

Outgoing and always eager to meet new people and to exchange, I've been a traveller myself and spent more than 20 years living in Brazil, Sao Paulo, where I set up my own business, worked as a professional photographer and learned new languages. Today, I've settled back in my beloved home city, Nice, and am very happy to present its secret beauties and magnificient Cote d'Azur region with other travellers!

MY SERVICES

Visiting South of France sounds like a dream...
What about visiting the Cote d'Azur with a local guide who takes care of everything for you?

Guided Tours

Let me be your guide during a day, maybe two or even your entire stay and show you all the must-sees but also the less known places that are also worth a visit.
...
The city of Nice as well as Monaco, Saint-Tropez and Cannes all hold their secret gems that are off the beaten tracks.

From restaurants, monuments, museums, activities, points of view to the narrow little streets from the old city, I'll bring you to the best places that the locals choose.

I can also adapt the tour depending on your centers of interest (history, art, architecture, photography, sports etc...)

Transport

Visiting Nice but don't have a car?
Part-time professional chauffeur for a Monegasque company, I can drive you around places in my own company registered vehicle. If coming by plane I can also come to pick you up and/or drop you off at the airport.

Reservations

Want to travel without the hassle of booking everything yourself?
I can assist you with all the bookings you'll need during your stay. Flights, hotels, activities, restaurants and more. Just reach out and let me know your preferences!

Photography

Former professional photographer, I'd be pleased to take some shots along our journey together, discovering the Cote d'Azur so that you get some good looking photos to keep.
"A photograph is a souvenir of a memory. It is not a moment. It is the looking at the photograph that becomes the moment. Your own moment." David Levithan
